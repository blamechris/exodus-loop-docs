# Carrier Types

The carrier is your mobile base and flagship. You select your carrier type at the start of each run, with the choice persisting across runs.

## Player Carrier Types (4)

| Type | Name | Icon | ATK | DEF | SPD | Hull | Hangar | PD | Detection |
|------|------|------|-----|-----|-----|------|--------|----|----|
| SCOUT | Scout Carrier | ◇ | 0 | 1 | 3 | 6 | 3 | 0 | 3 |
| STANDARD | Standard Carrier | ◈ | 1 | 2 | 1 | 10 | 4 | 1 | 2 |
| BATTLE | Battlecarrier | ◆ | 2 | 3 | 0 | 14 | 4 | 2 | 1 |
| TENDER | Fleet Tender | ◎ | 1 | 1 | 1 | 8 | 6 | 1 | 2 |

### Scout Carrier
**Fast and agile with extended sensors.**
- Highest speed (3) and detection range (3)
- Lowest hull (6) and no point defense
- Best for: Hit-and-run tactics, early detection, reconnaissance-heavy playstyles

### Standard Carrier
**Balanced performance for all situations.**
- Moderate stats across the board
- Default choice for new players
- Best for: Learning the game, adaptable strategies

### Battlecarrier
**Heavy armor and firepower at the cost of mobility.**
- Highest hull (14), ATK (2), DEF (3), and point defense (2)
- Zero speed and minimal detection
- Best for: Defensive playstyles, holding positions, tanking damage

### Fleet Tender
**Large hangar capacity for maximum squadron deployment.**
- Largest hangar (6 squadrons)
- Lower combat stats than Standard
- Best for: Squadron-focused strategies, flexibility in deployment

---

## Stat Explanations

| Stat | Description |
|------|-------------|
| ATK | Damage dealt when carrier attacks |
| DEF | Damage reduction from incoming attacks |
| SPD | Movement range per turn |
| Hull | Hit points before carrier is destroyed |
| Hangar | Maximum squadrons that can be stored |
| PD | Point Defense - destroys low-HP enemies approaching carrier |
| Detection | Range at which enemy movements are revealed |

---

## Carrier Selection

- Selected on the Fleet Setup screen before starting a run
- Choice persists between runs (saved to meta state)
- All carrier types available from the start (no unlock required)
- Upgrades like `reinforced_hull_1/2` add bonus HP to all carrier types

---

## Enemy Carriers

Enemy carriers have fixed stats but variable behavior traits:

**Base Stats:** ATK 1, DEF 2, SPD 1, Hull 10, Hangar 4

**Behavior Traits:**
| Trait | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| AGGRESSIVE | -50% retreat chance, +20% attack priority |
| DEFENSIVE | +30% hold-near-carrier behavior |
| HUNTER | Focuses exclusively on player carrier |
| CAUTIOUS | +40% retreat chance |
| STATIC | 80% hold position, 20% advance |
| SUICIDAL | 0% retreat, +1 ATK |

Enemy carriers reinforce at 50% base chance per turn when squadrons are destroyed.
